Privacy Policy — SleepWave

Last updated: June 08, 2026

SleepWave ("the app") is built to respect your privacy. This policy explains what information the app does and does not collect.

Information we collect

The app itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to us. All of your content and activity in the app is processed and stored locally on your device. We have no servers and no account system.

On-device storage

The app uses your device's local storage to remember your settings and saved data so they persist between sessions. This data never leaves your device and is removed if you uninstall the app.

Advertising

The app displays ads through Google AdMob. To serve ads, Google and its partners may collect and use certain information, such as a device advertising identifier, approximate or precise location (if permitted), IP address, and app-interaction data. This is handled by Google, not by us. You can learn more and manage your choices in Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy, and in Google's "How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services" page: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
